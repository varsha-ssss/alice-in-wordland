Instructions

Alice in Wordland

1

Game Objective

Use the clues to transform the starting word step-by-step until you reach the final word. Each correct answer helps Alice climb higher up the ladder. Get her to the top and escape the Queen of Hearts!

2

How to Play

  • You'll start with a base word and a clue.
  • Use the clue to change the base word into a new word.
  • Each correct transformation moves you up one step!
Example:
Base Word: PINT
Clue: A Valentine's Day color
Answer: PINK

In Level 1, you will change one letter of a three letter word. In Level 2, you will change one letter of a four letter word. In Level 3, you will change two letters of a five letter word.

3

Timer & Controls

  • The timer starts automatically when the game begins.
  • Press Pause anytime to stop the game (this disables typing).
  • Press Start to continue.

4

Track Your Progress

Watch the streak tracker at the top left increase as you play the game as you answer correctly on the first try. The tracker shows both your current streak as well as your best. After you reach the final word, you win and Alice escapes! The timer stops automatically, and you can then reach the Congratulations page, where your best streak and final time will be displayed.
